# Noor Boutique – Exchange Policy

1. Exchanges are accepted within 10 days of delivery date.
2. Size exchanges are subject to availability of the requested size.
3. Color exchanges are subject to availability of the requested color.
4. Customer is responsible for return shipping costs for exchanges.
5. Noor Boutique covers shipping for the replacement item at no extra cost.
6. Only one exchange is permitted per order. No second exchanges.
7. Custom-stitched items are NOT eligible for exchange.
8. The exchanged item must be unused, unworn, and in original condition with tags.
9. If the requested size/color is not available, customer can choose a different product of equal or lesser value, or opt for a refund instead.
10. Exchange requests must include the order ID and the desired new size or color.
