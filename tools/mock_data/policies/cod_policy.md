# Taara Boutique – Cash on Delivery (COD) Policy

1. COD is available for orders with total value under ₹5000 only.
2. COD is NOT available for custom-stitched items.
3. COD is NOT available for orders shipping outside India.
4. If a customer refuses to accept COD delivery, the order will be automatically cancelled.
5. Refused COD orders are not eligible for re-delivery. Customer must place a new order with prepaid payment (UPI, card, or net banking).
6. Customers who refuse COD delivery 3 or more times will have COD permanently disabled for their account.
7. COD orders may take 1-2 additional business days for processing compared to prepaid orders.
8. A convenience fee of ₹49 is added to all COD orders.
