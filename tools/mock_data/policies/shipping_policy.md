# Noor Boutique – Shipping Policy

1. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days from the date of dispatch.
2. Express delivery is available for an additional ₹149 and takes 2-3 business days.
3. Free shipping on all orders above ₹1499.
4. Shipping charge of ₹99 applies to orders below ₹1499.
5. We ship to all locations within India. International shipping is not available currently.
6. A tracking link will be shared with the customer within 24 hours of dispatch via the same channel (Instagram DM or WhatsApp).
7. Noor Boutique is not responsible for delays caused by courier partners, natural disasters, or government restrictions.
8. If a shipment is not delivered within 15 business days of dispatch, customer can request a full refund or re-shipment.
9. Custom-stitched items require 7-10 additional business days for preparation before dispatch.
